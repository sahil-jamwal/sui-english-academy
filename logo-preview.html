New — answering "make it look premium"

Premium Highlight Options

Two ways to add shine on top of what's already there — tried on both the full trail logo (the one you screenshotted) and Icon 1, so you can see the effect either way. H1 — Glass Sheen: one diagonal streak of light across all the bars, like a glass or enamel badge. H2 — Top-Edge 3D: a bright cap on top of each bar, like a rounded bezel catching light — more subtle, more "solid metal/plastic" than "glass." Hover/tap to replay and watch the shine animate in after the bars finish rising.

Icon version — small sizes, social profiles, app icons

App Icon Treatments

Redesigned specifically to pop at small sizes, the way Instagram/Google/PhonePe icons do: strong background contrast, richer saturation, dimensional depth (soft top highlight + bottom shadow), thicker bars, and no dotted trail (it turns to noise below ~100px). Each treatment is shown as "bars + plane" and "bars only" — both at 40px, 80px and full size — on a light and a dark surrounding, so you can judge real-world visibility. This is the version that would be used for profile pictures, favicons and app-style icons; the full lockup below stays for the header and banners.
